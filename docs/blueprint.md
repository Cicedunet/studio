# **App Name**: Élégance & Bien-Être

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: Display Liz-Francine's bio, highlighting her roles as a nurse and distributor of high-end perfumes & cosmetics from Corrèze.
- AI-Powered Personalized Scent Profiler: Users answer a series of questions about their preferences, and an AI tool suggests perfumes from the available list that match their taste, guiding them to discover exceptional scents. This tool intelligently filters the product catalog based on the responses.
- Product Catalog Display: Embed a visually appealing, browsable version of the 'liste des parfums' and 'catalogues des produits' Google Drive folders within the site. Make it filterable and searchable.
- Online Boutique Integration: Provide a direct link to the Chogan online store, ensuring seamless navigation for product purchases.
- Business Opportunity Landing: Dedicated section describing the opportunity to start a business, linking to the registration page, along with testimonials.
- Current Offers: Dynamic display of current promotions and special offers, directly linked to the order form for easy access.
- Personalized Advice Contact: A prominent, easily accessible link to Liz-Francine's WhatsApp for personalized consultations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft lavender (#E6E6FA) evoking sophistication and the perfumes, as well as her identity as a healthcare worker.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F5F5) for a clean and elegant backdrop.
- Accent color: Dusty rose (#D8BFD8) to highlight important links and calls to action, complementing the lavender primary.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya' serif, conveying elegance and a contemporary feel.
- Use minimalist, elegant icons related to beauty, wellness, and fragrance, in the dusty rose accent color.
- Clean, modern layout with plenty of white space to give a sense of luxury and refinement.
- Subtle fade-in effects for elements as the user scrolls, and a smooth transition when navigating between sections.
